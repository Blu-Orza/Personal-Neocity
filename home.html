I'm Blubunno (Blu or Bun for short, whatever you feel fits me best) and I'm the webmaster of this little cave in the inmensiveness that is the internet nowadays. As a 2000s kid I often feel nostalgia of the old web days, everything felt more alive and human. Sadly I was too young to take an active part on that comunity, but now things have changed! This is a love letter for those old webs and blogs I loved so much as a kid, and for my younger self who would have died to have one of these.

I plan to use this place to practice my code while I keep track of my interest, proyects, and why not use it as a blog from time to time. Since this is a big proyect, and I plan to keep it for years to come, It'll be a eternal work in progress so you've been warned! Mainly this is a webspace made by myself for myself, but feel welcome to check it out if you feel curious enough. That being said, enjoy your stay!
